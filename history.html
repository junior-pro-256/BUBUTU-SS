OUR HISTORY

Foundation And Early years


Bubutu secondary school was established in 1989 by Anglican church of Makhonge as a community based institution operating in the primary section with only two classes that is s.1 and s.2 because of limited space and classroom blocks . Its founding purpose was to expand access to secondary Education for children from Economically disadvantaged backgrounds. At that time there was only Bumbo ss and Magale ss making it difficult for children to from places like Butiru, Nabitsikhi, Namboko and Bubutu leaving a significant gap in post-primary education

LeaderShip and Administration


Since it was established, Bubutu secondary school has been led by series of dedictated headteachers who have contributed to its stability and development. The longest serving headteacher was Mr. Mungau Patrick who was the founder headteacher and managed to serve the institute for 11 years (1989 to 2000) and laid a strong and academic foundations.

Growth Legacy And Academic Excellence

Bubutu secondary school has a rich history rooted in a community service, access to education and sustained academic development. Since its establishment, the school has grown from the modest community initiative into respected government -aided secondary school seving learners across Bugisu sub region and beyond.

Transition To Government-Aided Status


Bubutu Secondary School transitioned into a government-aided institution in 2011, fully supported and regulated by the Ministry of Education and sports. This transition enhanced access to public resources, Teachers deployment and National Education programs.
This school align with national development goals, including human capital development skills acquisition and education reforms outlined in uganda vision 2040 and subsequent national development goals.

Academic Growth and Expansion


The school initially offered Ordinary Level (O'LEVEL) education only. Due to its growing reputations descipline and academic performance, Bubutu secondary school was upgraded in 2014 to offer Advanced level(A'level) education. This milestone marked a significant turning point, enabling learners to pursue higher academic pathway within their local community.
Enrollment steadily increased over the year under the leadership of Mr.Kimono Paul, reflecting growing trust from parents and improved academic outcomes. From an initial enrollment of 10 students and only two teachers. The school has now grown to 2300 learners as of 2026, demonstrating sustained institutional growth and development.

Bubutu Sec Sch Today


Today, Bubutu Secondary School stands as respected and best performing government-aided,mixed day, O'level and A'Level secondary school in the region offering. With a serene learning environment, committed staff, and ongoing infrastructure development. The school remains dedicated to its founding purpose providing quality education grounded in affordable academic services to all learners, innovation,technology and self reliance.

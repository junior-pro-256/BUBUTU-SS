ABOUT US
Who Are We

Bubutu secondary school is a mixed ordinary level and Advanced level secondary school lecated in Bubutu, namisindwa District, approximately 400m from Bubutu trading center along the Mbale-Lwakhakha High way.

Our school provides alearner-centered Education that combines academic excellence with moral values, leadership development creatitvity, innovation, and practical life skills.
We strive to create a learning environment where every student is valued,challenged, and supported to succeed.

ACADEMICS

Our curriculum follows the the Uganda National Curriculum and prepares learners for success in the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) and Uganda Advanced certificate of Education (UACE) examinations. We offer both Arts and science programs,supported by experienced teachers continuous assessments t help learners reach there academic goals.
